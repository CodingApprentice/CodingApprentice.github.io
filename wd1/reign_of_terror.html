Robespierre

During the early 1790s robespierre became the hero of the poor because of his opposition the king, the Noble's, the church and the high food prices. When the Jacobins came to control the convention, a special group of 12 men was appointed from the country to weed out the counter revolutionaries. Under Robespierre, the work of the committee of public safety came to be known as the reign of Terror. This was because under this rain many people were killed, eventually robespierre himself.
